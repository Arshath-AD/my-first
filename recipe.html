Let's cook
chicken fajitas

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb bonelessskinless chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, sour cream, shredded cheese, cilantro, salsa

step-by-step instructions

Preparation (15 minutes)

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, cumin,
    and cayenne pepper (if using).
  2. Add chicken to the marinade and mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least
    30 minutes or up to 2 hours.

Cooking (20 minutes)

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Rove chicken from marinade, letting excess liquid drip off.
  3. Add chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through,about
    5-7 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  4. In the same skillet, add sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook until tender,
    about 5 minutes.
  5. Add cooked chicken back into the skillet and stir to combine.

Assbly (5 minutes)

  1. Warm tortillas by wrapping th in a damp paper towel and microwaving for
    20-30 seconds.
  2. Assble fajitas by placing chicken and vegetable mixture onto tortillas.
  3. Add desired toppings and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • Use any color bell peppers or combination.

  • Substitute chicken with shrimp or steak for a different twist.
  • Serve with your favorite salsa or hot sauce.
